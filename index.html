Welcome!

Join us in our Spring 2025 seminar COMPSCI 692PA on AI Security and Privacy.

New capabilities of language and diffusion models enable applications that interact with users across different modalities, perform independent actions, and leverage external tools. In this seminar, we will invite leading experts to discuss cutting-edge research in advancing the safety and trustworthiness of GenAI. This is a 1-credit class where students are expected to attend talks in person, participate in discussions, and write 2 thorough critiques of the speaker's presented work. Please join our Slack channel to stay up-to-date with the latest news and events.
