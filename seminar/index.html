Join us in our Fall 2025 seminar: COMPSCI 692PA on AI Safety, Privacy and Security!

New capabilities of language and diffusion models enable applications that interact with users across different modalities, perform independent actions, and leverage external tools. In this seminar, we will invite leading experts to discuss cutting-edge research in advancing the safety and trustworthiness of GenAI. This is a 1-credit class where students are expected to attend talks and participate in discussions. Please join our Slack channel to stay up-to-date with the latest news and events.

Schedule

  • The seminar will take place every Monday from 10am to 11pm ET (there may be some exceptions, see specific date below).
  • You can attend virtually via Zoom or in person at CS 142.
  • The schedule below is still being updated and maybe subject to changes, so check back occasionally.
  • If you are interested in giving a seminar talk, please email or send us a Slack message (our contact info can be found here).
